Where will ITP's Program in Shanghai be located?
ITP's program in Shanghai will initially be located on the campus of East China Normal University, one of the country’s premier institutions of higher learning, located to the northwest of the city center. The sprawling, green campus hosts many facilities such as a large library, classroom and administrative buildings. ITP's facilities will also feature a workshop for physical prototyping: a machine shop, firmware programming stations, electronics prototyping tools, and digital fabrication. An equipment room giving students access to digital photography, video, and audio equipment will also be on site. NYU's Shanghai main campus is scheduled to open in September 2014 and will be located in downtown Pudong (within the Lujiazui Finance and Trade Zone) on Century Boulevard, minutes from two of the tallest buildings in the world and Century Park, the largest park in Shanghai. The campus will include new dormitories, access to complete athletic facilities and a new state-of-the art academic building. Classrooms will allow students in Shanghai to collaborate and communicate with fellow students throughout the Global Network. NYU Shanghai's library will have 30,000 volumes and be part of, and connected to the libraries of NYU NY and NYU Abu Dhabi. The campus will also include performance and cultural spaces, a bookstore, and dining facilities.
Will ITP's Program in Shanghai be identical to ITP in New York?
Students in ITP’s Shanghai program will follow the same degree requirements as ITP’s New York students in order to earn the 60 credit Master of Professional Studies degree (http://itp.nyu.edu/sigs/program/degree-requirements) Courses are taught in English. Incoming students to ITP’s Shanghai program will receive the same Foundation Courses as ITP students in New York. Tier Two courses will not only reflect the diverse areas of research and innovation inherent to the mission of ITP, but will also be developed to reflect the unique opportunities available in Shanghai.
Can students in IT'S Shanghai Program study in New York? Can ITP New York students study in Shanghai?
All incoming Fall 2013 ITP Shanghai and ITP New York students can apply to study abroad at the sister site in their third semester, Fall 2014.
Tuition and Fees?
Tuition is uniform on all campuses within the NYU Network. Financial aid is available at all of the campuses and schools. There will be significant financial aid available for qualified students at ITP’s Shanghai Program. For information regarding NYU Tuition and Fees, please visit the Bursar’s Office. Information can be found under ”Tisch School of the Arts (Graduate)”: http://www.nyu.edu/bursar/tuition.fees/
